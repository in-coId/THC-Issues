## About

**THC**, also known as **The Harmonic Client**, is an audio-reactive Minecraft visualizer mod built around music, particles, HUD visualizers, listening parties, and customizable player-driven effects.

Turn your music into animated particle systems, glowing geometry, responsive HUD widgets, lyrics, cover art, and immersive player-centered audio visuals.

## Features

* Audio-reactive particles and geometry visualizers
* HUD media player with cover art, playback controls, progress, volume, and visualizer styles
* Multiple visualizer types, including bars, waveform, oscilloscope, dot sphere, network, rain, float, emerge, fly, cloud, cube, pyramid, sphere, and true sphere
* Custom themes with color presets, color picker, RGB/hex input, opacity controls, and particle color slots
* Lyrics HUD with scrolling lyric display
* Song queue support with automatic preparation for upcoming tracks
* Listening party system with party codes, party permissions, member controls, and shared queues
* Player music controls for muting or lowering other players
* Configurable HUD scale, UI scale, blur, animations, fonts, and layout options
* Designed for compatibility with Fabric, NeoForge, Sodium, Iris, and dynamic lighting setups

---

## Music Support

THC is designed to support music from common sources such as:

* YouTube links and searches
* SoundCloud links
* Spotify metadata and fallback lookup
* Local or system output capture where supported

The mod can display track information, artist names, cover art, progress, queue state, and lyrics when available.

---

## Customization

THC is built around personalization. Players can adjust:

* Particle count, size, distance, height, speed, bass reaction, BPM reaction, drift, gravity, and glow
* Visualizer style, animation type, world shape, and optional second visualizer
* HUD layout, media player layout, lyrics HUD, visualizer HUD, opacity, and positioning
* Theme colors, glow colors, panel colors, particle colors, and custom hex/RGB values
* Fonts, UI scale, HUD scale, background blur, and animations

---

## Multiplayer

THC includes multiplayer-focused controls so players can manage how they experience other people’s music and visuals.

* Mute or lower other players’ music
* Disable other players’ particles or HUD song display locally
* Join listening parties with shared queues
* Use party leader controls for skipping, locking, muting, kicking, banning, or limiting member actions

## Compatibility

* Minecraft 1.21.1
* Fabric 1.21.1
* NeoForge 21.1.233 for Minecraft 1.21.1
* Sodium compatible
* Iris shader compatible
* Designed with dynamic lights support in mind

## Notes

THC is actively being developed. Some provider features, synced lyrics, cover art, party features, dynamic lighting, and audio capture behavior may vary depending on platform, installed dependencies, and server configuration.

Server owners can configure restrictions for music playback, particles, HUD visibility, listening parties, and player permissions.
